# Narrative Principles

This document defines what Mitty & Co is, what it believes, what it refuses to become, and how all brand decisions should be framed.

## Core Narrative

Mitty & Co is a handcrafted terracotta jewellery studio that bridges traditional Indian artistry with modern expression.

The brand begins with a simple act: an artisan molds earthen clay by hand. Every piece is sun-dried, baked, and painted by a single artist whose background in classical Indian music informs the rhythm, patience, and soul of her visual art. 

The pieces cannot be exactly repeated. Each creation is an exploration of shape and color. 

## Brand Promise

Mitty & Co gives people a way to wear traditional Indian earth in a form that fits seamlessly into a modern life. 

## The One-Sentence Position

Mitty & Co creates soulful, one-of-a-kind terracotta jewellery, sculpted by hand to bring traditional Indian artistry into modern everyday wear.

## What Mitty & Co Is

Mitty & Co is:
- A celebration of earthen clay.
- A bridge between the traditional and the modern.
- A labor of love by a solo woman artisan.
- Wearable art.
- Slow fashion, mindful and unhurried.
- Eco-conscious and skin-safe.

## What Mitty & Co Is Not

Mitty & Co is not:
- Mass-produced or factory-made.
- Fast fashion.
- Perfect, uniform, or mold-cast.
- A corporate jewelry brand.
- Just "traditional wear" restricted to sarees.

## The Main Character

The main character is the intersection of the Artisan's hands and the Earth. 
All surfaces should quietly respect the time and soul poured into the craft.

## Narrative Pillars

### 1. The Heritage of Clay
Terracotta is a core part of Indian heritage. We honor the material without being trapped by old constraints.

### 2. Traditional Clay. Modern Expression.
The jewellery is designed for all ages, mentalities, and wardrobes. It belongs just as much with a contemporary dress as it does with a handloom saree.

### 3. The Unrepeatable Nature of Art
We do not use molds, and we mix our paints by hand. "Once a design is done, it is done." This makes every customer the sole owner of their specific piece of wearable art.

### 4. A Classical Soul
The founder is a classical vocalist. Her art and her music are intertwined. Both require patience, tradition, and personal interpretation.
