# PRODUCT.md

## Product

**goonjaa** is a handcrafted terracotta jewellery storefront for one-of-a-kind pieces shaped and painted by a solo woman artisan.

## Register

Brand-led commerce. The site must sell jewellery, but the first job is to make the craft feel specific, human, and worth waiting for.

## Audience

- People who want Indian craft without feeling limited to traditional occasions.
- Wearers who pair jewellery with sarees, dresses, and everyday wardrobes.
- Gift buyers planning thoughtful pieces or small-batch repeat orders.
- Customers who value slow fashion and handmade variation.

## Purpose

Help visitors understand the craft, browse collections, choose pieces, and place cart, checkout, contact, or bulk-order requests without making the studio feel like a factory.

## Brand Truths

- Every piece starts with earthen clay.
- The work is sculpted, dried, baked, and painted by hand.
- The pieces are unrepeatable because there are no molds.
- The founder's classical music background is part of the studio's patience and rhythm.
- Real product and studio photography should eventually carry the site.

## Voice

Warm, grounded, artistic, and clear. The copy should sound like a careful studio note, not like a corporate catalogue.

## Anti-References

- Factory language.
- Aggressive sale language.
- Generic luxury copy.
- Perfect, uniform, mass-produced claims.
- Placeholder text that reads like a template.

## UX Principles

- Keep commerce clear, especially cart, checkout, forms, and bulk-order timing.
- Make handmade variation feel intentional.
- Use lowercase `goonjaa` everywhere.
- Preserve the current routes, data model, checkout behavior, and font assets unless a future plan says otherwise.
