# Voice

Voice is the stable personality of **goonjaa**. Tone changes by situation, but the voice stays warm, grounded, and human.

## Voice Summary

goonjaa sounds like a sunlit home studio: clay on the table, color mixed by hand, music in the background.

## Voice Qualities

### 1. Artistic But Accessible

The brand respects classical and traditional roots while welcoming modern interpretation.

- Sounds like: "Traditional clay. Modern expression."
- Doesn't sound like: "Only for cultural purists."

### 2. Human And Honest

The product is made by a person, not a machine.

- Sounds like: "No two handmade pieces are exactly the same."
- Doesn't sound like: "Precision engineered to perfection."

### 3. Patient

The craft takes time: drying, baking, painting, and correcting.

- Sounds like: "Slow fashion, carefully molded."
- Doesn't sound like: "Instant delivery, rapid manufacturing."

## Voice In Practice

### Buttons

Buttons should feel curated.

- Good: "Explore Collection", "Our Story", "Inquire Now", "Add to Cart"
- Avoid: "Buy Now", "Click Here", "Submit"

### Placeholders

Placeholders should be useful and gentle.

- Good: "Tell us about the colors you are looking for."
- Avoid: "Enter Details"

### Empty States

Empty states should normalize absence and guide users back to the shop.

- Good: "Looks like you have not added any terracotta art pieces to your cart yet. Let us find you something special."
- Avoid: "Your cart is empty."
