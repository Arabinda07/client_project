# Voice

Voice is the stable personality of Mitty & Co. Tone changes by situation, but voice stays consistent.

## Voice Summary

Mitty & Co sounds artistic, warm, grounded, and deeply human. It feels like stepping into a peaceful, sunlit artist's studio.

## The Voice Qualities

### 1. Artistic but Accessible
The brand respects its classical and traditional roots but welcomes modern interpretation. 
- Sounds like: "Traditional clay. Modern expression."
- Doesn't sound like: "Only for cultural purists."

### 2. Human and Honest
The product is made by a person, not a machine.
- Sounds like: "No two handmade pieces are exactly the same."
- Doesn't sound like: "Precision engineered to perfection."

### 3. Patient
The craft takes time (drying, baking, painting). The voice should feel unhurried.
- Sounds like: "Slow fashion, carefully molded."
- Doesn't sound like: "Instant delivery, rapid manufacturing."

## Voice In Practice

### Buttons
Buttons should feel curated.
- Good: "Explore Collection", "Our Story", "Inquire Now", "Add to Cart"
- Avoid: "Buy Now", "Click Here", "Submit"

### Placeholders
Placeholders should be helpful and gentle.
- Good: "Tell us about the colors you're looking for..."
- Avoid: "Enter Details"

### Empty States
Empty states should normalize absence and guide back to the shop.
- Good: "Looks like you haven't added any terracotta art pieces to your cart yet. Let's find you something special."
- Avoid: "Your cart is empty."
